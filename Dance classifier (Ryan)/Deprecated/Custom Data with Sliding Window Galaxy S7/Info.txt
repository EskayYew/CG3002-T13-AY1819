Readings are from linear acceleration of onboard accelerometer of Samsung Galaxy S7

Data is sampled every 50ms.

Each block has 40 datapoints (2 seconds). Sliding window occurs every 0.5s

The following features will be attached at the end of each block
max_x
min_x
range_x
max_y
min_y
range_y
max_z
min_z
range_z
